Our Services

At BuildCon, we offer a wide range of construction and contracting services to meet the needs of our clients.

New construction

Our new construction services encompass everything from design and planning to construction and finishing touches. In addition, we also provide comprehensive project management to ensure that the construction process runs smoothly and is completed on time and within budget.

Renovations and remodeling

Whether you're looking to update your home or business with a fresh new look, or need to make necessary repairs, our renovations and remodeling services can help. We'll work with you to create a plan that meets your needs and fits your budget, and deliver exceptional results every time.

Commercial construction

From office buildings and retail spaces to industrial complexes and more, our commercial construction services are designed to meet the needs of businesses of all sizes. We'll work with you to create a space that meets your unique needs and supports your business goals.

Our Projects

As a premier construction and contracting company, we are proud to have completed some of the most iconic skyscrapers in the city.

The Summit Tower

This impressive structure stands at 80 stories tall and is the tallest building in the city. Our team worked tirelessly to bring this vision to life, using the latest technology and expert craftsmanship to create a building that is both functional and aesthetically stunning.

The Horizon Tower

The Horizon Tower is a 60-story skyscraper that features a unique design inspired by the city's skyline. Our team worked closely with the architects to ensure that the building was not only visually striking, but also energy-efficient and environmentally friendly.

The Skyline Plaza

The Skyline Plaza is a mixed-use development that includes both residential and commercial spaces. With 50 stories of luxury apartments, retail space, and office space, this building is a true landmark in the city. Our team was responsible for all aspects of the construction.
